Pancakes

Description

Nothing beats pancakes!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp butter or margarine, melted

Steps

  1. Preheat a griddle or frying pan over medium heat and grease lightly
  2. In a medium-sized bowl, stir together flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar
  3. Add milk, eggs, and butter - stir together just until flour is moistened
  4. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake on griddle, spread out to make a 5 inch circle
  5. Cook until bubbles form and start to pop on the surface, and edges appear dry
  6. Turn pancake with a wide spatula to lightly brown other side
