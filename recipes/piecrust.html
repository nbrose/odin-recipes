Pie Crust

Description

Make delicious, flaky pie crust from scratch with this easy recipe. (Makes four crusts.)

Ingredients

  • 5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 3 tsp sugar
  • 1 2/3 cups shortening
  • Ice water

Steps

  1. Combine flour, salt, and sugar in a large bowl
  2. Cut in shortening
  3. Stir in just enough water to form a stiff dough, adding water a tbsp at a time
  4. Divide dough into four pieces
  5. Roll out each piece between two sheets of wax paper
  6. Dough can be frozen for up to a month
