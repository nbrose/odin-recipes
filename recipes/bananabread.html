Banana Bread

Description

What to do with those overripe bananas? Try this classic banana bread!

Ingredients

  • 3 large ripe bananas
  • 1 egg
  • 4 tbsp butter, melted and cooled
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp each salt and baking soda

Steps

  1. Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan, set aside
  2. Preheat oven to 325 degrees
  3. In a large bowl, mash bananas
  4. Add eggs and butter
  5. Add dry ingredients, mix
  6. Pour batter into loaf pan and bake for 55-60 min
